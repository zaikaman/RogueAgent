### Visual Style
- **Color Palette**: Noir black (#0a0a0a), Teal/Cyan accents (#00ff9d, #00b4d8)
- **Typography**: Bold, clean sans-serif (Space Grotesk or similar)
- **Effects**: Glitch transitions, particle animations, subtle screen flickers
- **Music**: Dark electronic/synthwave ambient (low intensity, builds at key moments)

### Screen Recording Tips
1. Use 1920x1080 or higher resolution
2. Hide bookmarks bar for cleaner look
3. Enable smooth scrolling
4. Pre-load all pages to avoid loading delays
5. Use a consistent, slow cursor movement
